Kits for Kids

These initiative focuses on providing free self-care kits, with a particular emphasis on serving low-income individuals and people of color. Recognizing the disproportionate health risks and financial stress faced by these communities, we aim to alleviate their burden by offering self-care resources. By addressing cultural barriers and providing accessible tools and knowledge, we empower individuals to take care of their mental and emotional well-being.
